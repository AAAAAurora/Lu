MTE-SLAM: Multi-Tier Feature Fusion for Efficient Neural Semantic SLAM

论文 1 · ICRA 2026 · 神经语义 SLAM Publication 1 · ICRA 2026 · Neural Semantic SLAM

打开论文 PDF Open PDF

论文概述 Overview

MTE-SLAM 面向 RGB-D 语义 SLAM 任务，提出多层级特征融合框架，将几何、语义与外观信息在全局和局部尺度上协同建模。该方法通过全局跨模态注意力增强场景级语义一致性，并利用局部增强与动态门控改善物体边界、细小结构和语义区域的清晰度。 MTE-SLAM is designed for RGB-D semantic SLAM. It introduces a multi-tier feature fusion framework that jointly models geometry, semantics, and appearance at global and local scales. Global cross-modal attention improves scene-level semantic consistency, while local enhancement and dynamic gating improve object boundaries, fine structures, and semantic regions. 为降低在线建图中的冗余特征累积，论文进一步设计特征冗余抑制模块，根据特征重要性和时序变化自适应压缩表示。在 Replica 和 ScanNet 数据集上的实验显示，该方法在跟踪、重建、语义分割与运行效率之间取得良好平衡。 To reduce redundant feature accumulation during online mapping, the paper further designs a feature redundancy suppressor that adaptively compresses representations according to feature importance and temporal variation. Experiments on Replica and ScanNet show a strong balance among tracking, reconstruction, semantic segmentation, and runtime efficiency.

关键贡献 Key Contributions

  • 提出多层级特征融合机制，在全局语义一致性和局部边界细节之间取得平衡。 Introduces multi-tier feature fusion to balance global semantic consistency and local boundary details.
  • 通过动态门控融合不同模态信息，使几何、语义和外观特征根据场景位置自适应协同。 Uses dynamic gating to adaptively coordinate geometry, semantic, and appearance features across scene locations.
  • 设计特征冗余抑制模块，降低在线神经隐式 SLAM 中的冗余特征累积和计算开销。 Designs a feature redundancy suppressor to reduce redundant feature accumulation and computation in online neural implicit SLAM.
